Community Agreement

Be Curious About the Experience of Others
Minority genders have a different experience on the court - be constantly mindful. Ask questions that help you understand where people are coming from.

Fair Play
Prioritize care over competitiveness. If anyone falls on the court, we pause until they're up & moving. Match the pace of who you're playing. Do everything you can to make the game accessible & fun for everyone. #1 priority is to take care of yourself on the court.

Proactively Communicate
Speak your truth clearly & directly. Ask questions for clarity & understanding.

Take Space, Make Space
Be assertive & fully yourself. Call it if someone fouls you. Notice & elevate quieter voices.
